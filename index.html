About Butter and Love

Welcome to butter and love, where we specialize in creating delicious cookies that are made with care and passion. Located in Palm Beach County, FL, our bakery is dedicated to providing our customers with high-quality treats that are sure to satisfy any sweet tooth.

With a focus on using the finest ingredients and traditional baking techniques, we take pride in producing cookies that are not only tasty but also made with love. Whether you're looking for a classic chocolate chip cookie or a unique flavor combination, butter and love has something for everyone.
